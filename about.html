About CFD

Our Story

Charleston Fashion & Design is a refined gathering celebrating fashion, interiors, craftsmanship, and contemporary Southern design.

Mission

Elevating Southern design on a national stage

Charleston Fashion & Design exists to champion the designers, makers, and creative voices of the American South. We believe that great design is rooted in place, and that Charleston — with its deep history, architectural beauty, and thriving creative community — is the ideal setting for a new kind of fashion and design event.

CFD is not a loud festival. It is a polished cultural event that brings together local and national talent in an atmosphere of intimacy, craft, and genuine conversation. Our programming bridges fashion, interiors, architecture, and the decorative arts, reflecting the way design is actually lived and experienced in the South.

We are committed to building a year-round platform — not just a single weekend — that supports emerging designers, connects them with mentors and patrons, and creates lasting relationships between brands, institutions, and the community.

Vision

A quieter, more elevated model for fashion events

We envision CFD as the preeminent platform for Southern fashion and design — a destination event that draws national attention while remaining deeply connected to Charleston's character. Our goal is to prove that fashion events can be thoughtful, intimate, and culturally rich without sacrificing ambition or scale.

By 2028, CFD will be recognized alongside the most respected regional design events in the country, offering designers a launchpad and audiences an experience they cannot find anywhere else.

Location

Why Charleston

Charleston is a city where history, architecture, and creative culture converge. Its cobblestone streets, historic homes, and vibrant arts scene provide a backdrop that no other American city can replicate. The city's hospitality tradition — warm, gracious, and deeply personal — naturally lends itself to the kind of event we are building.

The Lowcountry has long been a center of craft and making, from sweetgrass basketry to indigo dyeing, ironwork to furniture design. CFD honors these traditions while looking forward, connecting heritage craft with contemporary fashion and design practice.

Charleston's creative economy is growing rapidly. New design studios, boutiques, and galleries are opening alongside established institutions. CFD provides a platform that unites these energies under one cohesive seasonal program, attracting visitors, press, and industry attention to the city's design community.
