Events

Three signature gatherings across the year, each designed to celebrate a different dimension of fashion, design, and Southern craft.

Flagship Event

CFD 2026

March 19 – 22, 2026

The flagship multi-day gathering with runway presentations, designer talks, private brand dinners, and cultural programming across Charleston. CFD 2026 is the anchor event of the year, bringing together local and national designers for four days of immersive fashion and design.

Thursday, March 19
Opening Night Reception — Welcome cocktails and preview of featured collections at The Gaillard Center.
Friday, March 20
Morning designer talks and panel conversations. Evening runway presentations featuring six emerging and established designers.
Saturday, March 21
Main runway event and designer showcases. Patron-only dinner hosted at a historic King Street venue. Craft workshop sessions throughout the day.
Sunday, March 22
Closing brunch and awards ceremony. Final designer pop-up markets and farewell gatherings.
Venue: The Gaillard Center & select venues across the Historic District, Charleston, SC

Summer Series

Design Salon

June – August 2026

An intimate event series spotlighting interiors, architecture, craftsmanship, and the makers shaping Southern design. Design Salon unfolds across the summer months with curated evenings that bring together designers, collectors, and enthusiasts.

Maker Spotlights
Monthly evening gatherings featuring artisan demonstrations, studio visits, and conversations with Southern makers.
Interior Design Tours
Exclusive access to private residences and newly designed spaces across Charleston, led by the architects and designers behind them.
Craft & Material Workshops
Hands-on workshops exploring textiles, ceramics, woodworking, and other craft traditions of the Lowcountry.
Venues: Rotating locations including private studios, galleries, and historic properties across Charleston.

Winter Program

Holiday Atelier

November – December 2026

A curated seasonal program with trunk shows, collectible objects, and Charleston-hosted gatherings for patrons and partners. The Holiday Atelier transforms select venues into intimate shopping and cultural experiences during the holiday season.

Designer Trunk Shows
Curated presentations from emerging and established designers offering exclusive holiday collections and one-of-a-kind pieces.
Collectible Objects Market
A refined marketplace showcasing artisan goods, handcrafted jewelry, ceramics, textiles, and design objects from Southern and national makers.
Patron Gatherings
Private evening receptions in historic Charleston homes, with seasonal food, live music, and intimate conversations with designers.
Venues: King Street boutiques, historic homes, and partner retail locations throughout Charleston.
