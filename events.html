Event Details

Events

Charleston Fashion & Design 2026 centers on one refined evening in Charleston, followed the next morning by an invitation-only Charleston Fashion Brunch.

Friday, October 9

Main Presentation

The evening begins with a professionally produced runway presentation followed by a post-show reception in the Grand Ballroom at the Charleston Gaillard Center.

Venue

Grand Ballroom, Charleston Gaillard Center, 95 Calhoun Street, Charleston, SC 29401.

Audience

The event welcomes 300 guests, including Charleston’s design community and national fashion media.

Saturday, October 10 · By Invitation

Charleston Fashion Brunch

The following morning, invited guests and partners gather for a second social experience built around conversation, hospitality, and design.

Format

A polished morning gathering intended to extend the energy of the evening into the weekend.
