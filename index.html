The Venue

Grand Ballroom
Charleston Gaillard Center

The Grand Ballroom at the Gaillard Center is among the largest and most refined ballrooms in Charleston. The Center is home to Spoleto Festival USA, the Charleston Symphony, and the Lowcountry Jazz Festival, and welcomes more than 200,000 visitors a year.

Arriving at the Gaillard

95 Calhoun Street. There is garage parking on site. The Gaillard is walking distance from King Street and most of Charleston’s downtown hotels.
