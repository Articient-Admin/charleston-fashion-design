Sponsorships

Partner with a more polished Charleston platform. Position your brand at the center of Charleston's creative economy through fashion, design, and cultural programming.

Partner Categories

Four pillars of partnership

CFD brings together hospitality, retail, design, and cultural institutions under one elegant seasonal platform. Each category offers unique alignment opportunities.

Hospitality Partners

Hotels, Restaurants & Venues

Charleston's hospitality sector is the backbone of CFD. Partners in this category host events, provide accommodations for VIP guests, and integrate their venues into the CFD experience. From welcome dinners to patron receptions, hospitality partners create the atmosphere that defines the event.

  • • Event hosting and venue integration
  • • VIP guest accommodations
  • • Branded dining experiences
Retail & Luxury

Boutiques, Brands & Luxury Goods

Retail and luxury partners align with CFD's curated aesthetic. From trunk shows to pop-up activations, this category places brands directly in front of an engaged audience of design-conscious consumers, collectors, and tastemakers.

  • • Trunk show and pop-up opportunities
  • • Brand placement in event programming
  • • Exclusive gift bag and welcome kit inclusion
Design & Home

Interiors, Architecture & Craft

Design and home partners bring depth to CFD's programming. From showroom events to workshop collaborations, this category celebrates the intersection of fashion and the built environment that makes Charleston distinctive.

  • • Showroom integration and Design Salon participation
  • • Workshop and craft demonstration hosting
  • • Cross-category creative collaborations
Cultural Institutions

Museums, Galleries & Arts Organizations

Cultural institution partners enrich CFD with educational programming, exhibition spaces, and artistic credibility. These partnerships connect fashion and design to the broader arts and cultural conversation in Charleston.

  • • Exhibition and gallery event hosting
  • • Educational programming co-creation
  • • Artist-in-residence and cultural exchange
